Local Map World Map Quests Notes

Local Map - Shows a detailed map of the user's local area. Only explored areas appear.

World Map - Shows a map of the area. Locations discovered are marked; new locations appear when found. Markers guide the user to quest destinations, with secondary selectable markers and companion positions shown.

Quests - Shows any active or completed quests. Selecting a quest makes it active; markers appear on the local and world maps.

Notes - Keeps a record of discovered notes. Paper notes can be read, and holotapes can be played here.
